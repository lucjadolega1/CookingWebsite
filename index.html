Welcome to the cooking blog. Here you can find some tasty recipes.

Let's start with some interesting things like How did I start cooking? Hmm. The story isn't long. I started cooking during the covid-19 stay at home time. I had a lot of free time and I used it to practice my cooking skills. I can't call myself a chef but I think I make tasty dinners and my boyfriend doesn't complain.

You might be wondering if I continue cooking. The answer is yes but not as often as before.

Many of you might have a different idea of cooking. Some cook because they have to and some to relax or have fun. When I cook, I like to experiment. I mean, I like changing some ingredients or putting less amounts. I rarely save the recipes. I look at the recipes online and then just take a picture of what I done (including some prepared ingredients). It may seem crazy, but you know what? It always tastes great!
